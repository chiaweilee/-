## The Great Escape

To catch up with Full Moon Party, I have to travel 541km distance in 18 hours.

#### Day 1, afternoon

Departure from Koh Similan by Manta Queen 1.

#### Day 1, about 18:00

From Khao Lak Scuba Adventures to Phuket Bus Terminal 2 by Adventures' mini van.

#### Day 1, about 22:00

From Phuket Bus Terminal 2 to Krabi by final night bus to HAT YAI, arrived at about 01:00.

#### Day 2, about 07:00

Take Lomprayah's bus to Donsak Pier.

#### Day 2, about 09:00

Donsak Pier to Koh Phangan by Lomprayah's speed boat.

#### Day 2, about 12:00

Mini van from Thong Sala Pier to Rin Nai Beach.
